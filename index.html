About Me

Hi there! I'm Neha, a bright-eyed painter who aspires to be a front-end developer. I've seen similarities between the artistic process of painting and creating immersive digital experiences as I explore the field of web development. My desire to comprehend the complex dance between code and design led me to pursue a career in front-end development. I learn new techniques to combine utility and aesthetics with every line of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that I write in an effort to produce aesthetically pleasing and intuitive user interfaces.

When I'm not computing, I'm usually in my studio, lost in a world of hues and textures. For me, painting is more than simply a pastime; it's a way to express myself and find never-ending inspiration. My approach to front-end development is enhanced by the creativity and meticulousness developed from painting, which gives my projects a distinct flair.
